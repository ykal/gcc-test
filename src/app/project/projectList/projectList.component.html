Instructions to Enrol for a competition Click the plus button (+) to create a project. While filling the form, if you do not have a proposal ready, then just upload a document with one paragraph about the project and you can edit it later. After you upload your project, it will appear on the page within a box displaying your name. Click on ‘Enroll’ button and follow the steps. Once you finish filling the form and join, you can add your team members by clicking on the “Add Member” button on the Left side of the page. However, before you add your friends, they need to create an account on the website. Once they create their account, you can search them and add them as team members to your project. The instructions on how to search them are written on the particular page. To avoid confusion due to a similarity in name, we recommend that you should search them by their email address via which they created their Solve IT account.
Good Luck!
